How to Update Garmin 245?

Updating your Garmin 245 is quick and easy. Here’s how you can update your device:

  1. Connect to Garmin Express: First, ensure your Garmin 245 is connected to your computer via USB or paired with the Garmin Connect app on your smartphone.
  2. Check for Available Updates: Open Garmin Express or Garmin Connect and check for available firmware updates for your Garmin 245.

  4. Restart Your Device: After the update is installed, restart your Garmin 245 to apply the changes.
